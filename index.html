  5. V. Cuttlebase is a scientifictoolkit for the dwarf cuttlefish, Sepia bandensis
    1. To create an MRI-based 3D brain atlas for the dwarf cuttlefish, ex vivo magnetic resonance imaging of 8 adult dwarf cuttlefish brains (4 males, 4 females) at 50 µm isotropic resolution was performed. Deep learning techniques were used to improve manually-annoted brain masks that were then used to extract the brain regions for subsequent diffeomorphic registration using the ANTs toolbox.
